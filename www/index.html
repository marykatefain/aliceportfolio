About Alice Forde

A native to nowhere and a perpetual wanderer, Alice is currently a Philadelphia-based visual artist specializing in ink and oils. Her emotional portaits are known for their ability to demonstate what it means to truly be alive in an ever-dying world.

Alice is a connoisseur of unknown bands, and can regularly be found hoola-hooping at obscure shows. She is passionate about animal rights, and a fierce advocate for the oppressed — a theme which often shows through in her works.

Her minature lion friend, Silas, is her muse.
